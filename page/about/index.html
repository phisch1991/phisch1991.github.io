About

About me

I studied business information technology and IT management and work in IT for about an decade now. I love the DevOps approach (vs. separation of development and operations) and thinking in products (vs. thinking in projects). And I am interested in security related topics.

Why do I do this?

I am always willing to learn new cool stuff and share it with others. I like writing and explaining things to IT and non-IT people. I further think that in order to establish security discussions in DevOps teams, the term IT security needs some demystification.It is nothing only professional hackers can do in dark places with flashing LEDs, consuming Coke and Pizza, after gaining years of programming and cybersecurity experience. It is something that should be normal work of a DevOps team, just like programming languages, version control and dependency management. That’s why I decided to publish my own blog with topics crossing my way.

What to expect?

My intention is to write short and simple articles about several topics (such as tools simplifying my work or IT security). I do not want to write scientific publications, so I will stay on the level necessary to understand the topics, however I will do my best to deliver some further reading material in case you want to deep-dive :-)
